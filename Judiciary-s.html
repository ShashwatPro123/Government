Judicial State Government

Role of Government

The Judicial state government has a high court in every state if the Local court takes a wrong decision which the citizen does not like then he can go to the high court and if the high court takes a decision that the citizen does not like than he goes to the Supreme court and the decision of the Supreme Court is the final decision.

Influences of the Government on citizens life

The courts make the country conflict-free because they can solve any conflicts.
